The Viking View

Burnaby North's School Newspaper

To adapt to the extraordinary circumstances, the Viking View is publishing our third issue online. Our team of students has worked incredibly hard to digitize our student body's work, and is proud to present our platform for the voices and opinions of Burnaby North Secondary.

Our promise.

With the help of our sponsors, the Viking View will be donating all ad revenue from this issue to SD41's school meals program to help families affected by COVID-19 eat.
